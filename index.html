How inequality affects the labor aspect of women?

A study was carried out on the average number of hours that people work throughout the world, obtaining a summary of the hours paid and unpaid on average in all countries. Differences could be observed, according to the countries in which the studies were carried out. It was also seen that the age of people affects the number of hours they are paid

As we can see in the graph, there is a clear difference between the most developed countries versus the least developed ones. Those countries that have the most developed laws regarding human rights showed an abysmal difference between paid hours and unpaid hours, the latter being very few. It can be seen that the labor issue is something that affects both women and men
